[Andrea Allen](../../people/andrea-allen/) recently graduates from our Master’s in Complex Systems & Data Science after successfully defending her thesis entitled [Modeling the heterogeneous temporal dynamics of epidemics on networks](https://scholarworks.uvm.edu/graddis/1617/). She has now joined the [Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia](https://www.chop.edu/) as a Data Scientist. Congratulations Andrea!
