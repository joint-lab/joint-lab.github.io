Big news! Jean-Gabriel Young is becoming an assistant professor at the University of Vermont starting this fall! His primary appointment will be with the [Department of Mathematics and Statistics](https://www.uvm.edu/cems/mathstat) with a joint appointment at the [Larner College of Medicine](https://med.uvm.edu/), where he will join the modeling team of the [Translational Global Infectious Diseases Research Center (TGIR)](https://www.med.uvm.edu/tgircobre/home). He will also be on the faculty of the [Vermont Complex Systems Center](https://vermontcomplexsystems.org/) and of the [Computer Science department (secondary appointment)](https://www.uvm.edu/cems/cs).
