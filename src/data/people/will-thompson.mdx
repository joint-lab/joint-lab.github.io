## Biography

Will is a Ph.D. student and research assistant advised by Jean Gabriel Young and Laurent Hébert-Dufresne. His research centers on modeling and inferring the diffusion of information on social networks, studying binary state dynamics on higher order networks and data-driven inference of opinion dynamics models. 


From 2021 to 2022 he worked as a post-bac researcher at **Los Alamos National Labs** working on the [Coherent Captain  Mills](http://ccm.mit.edu/) searching for dark matter. In 2019 he worked for the **MITRE corporation** applying complex systems science to solve real world problems.
